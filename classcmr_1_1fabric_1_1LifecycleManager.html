Detailed Description

This node provides a service interface for activating and deactivating other nodes.

The activation service is on /<namespace>/activate and takes messages of cmr_msgs::srv::ActivateNode. The deactivation service is on /<namespace>/deactivate and takes messages of cmr_msgs::srv::DeactivateNode.
