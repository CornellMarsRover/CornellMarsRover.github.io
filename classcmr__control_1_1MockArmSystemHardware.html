Detailed Description

MockArmSystemHardware is a hardware interface for the arm that mocks effort and position control to each of the arm's joints.

No actual hardware commands are sent, but the arm's position and velocity are updated based on the commanded effort and position times a constant factor that's defined in the implementation.
